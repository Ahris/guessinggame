Guessing Game

Guessing Game

The computer will try to guess which move you will take.
Play against human players in two player and N player mode.

Single Player Two Players N Players

Rules

You can play against the computer, another player, or multiple other players. When you get to the game screen, enter the number of rounds you are assigned to play. Once the game is setup, you can take a turn by choosing an option (A, B or C) and submit your choice. Your choice and your opponent's choice will determine the amount of points you earn. Try to figure out the pattern and maximize your score! Good luck.
